My Projects

Brain Tumor prediction

Brain tumor prediction using deep learning focuses on early and accurate detection of tumors using medical images like MRI scans. Deep learning models, especially Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs), analyze these images to identify patterns that distinguish tumors from healthy tissue. The process involves preparing the data by improving image quality, training models like ResNet on labeled data, and evaluating their performance using metrics like accuracy. This approach helps improve diagnosis and supports medical professionals in providing better patient care.

Heart Disease detection

A heart disease detection project creates a system to predict the risk of heart problems by analyzing data like age, medical history, and test results. The goal is to help doctors find issues early and act quickly.The process involves collecting and cleaning patient data, then using it to train a program that spots patterns linked to heart disease. Popular methods include decision trees and neural networks. Once tested and verified, the program is made into an easy-to-use tool for doctors and patients to get quick risk assessments, improving heart health care.

Student Data Management System

A student data management system is used in schools to organize and handle information about students. It helps store details like names, IDs, grades, attendance, and activities in one secure place. This system makes it easy for teachers and staff to update, check, and analyze the data.The system ensures accuracy and saves time by creating reports and tracking attendance or academic progress. It is simple to use, so even non-technical users can navigate it. It can also work with other tools, like online learning platforms, to make managing student information more efficient.
